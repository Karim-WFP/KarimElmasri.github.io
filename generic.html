TMDB Movies Analysis Dashboard

Overview: The TMDB Movies Analysis Dashboard provides an interactive overview of movie performance across multiple dimensions. The analysis highlights the Top 20 movies ranked by profit and showcasing how actual revenue compares with revenue adjusted to 2010 dollars to account for inflation. A distribution of ROI by movie count reveals how profitability varies across productions, while a popularity versus average vote comparison uncovers the relationship between audience engagement and critical ratings. The dashboard also visualizes genre categories by total revenue, identifying top-earning genres, and pinpoints the most popular production company contributing to box-office success. All insights are dynamically filterable by year, rating, runtime, genre, and sub-genre, allowing users to explore trends and patterns from different analytical perspectives.
